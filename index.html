Kevin O'Leary Tribute

Kevin O'Leary

"There's only one Mr. Wonderful"

Here's a time line of Mr. Wonderful's life:

  • 1954 July 9: Kevin O'Leary is born in Montreal, Quebec, Canada
  • 1977-O'Leary completes his undergraduate studies at the University of Waterloo, earning a degree in Environmental Studies and Psychology.
  • 1980-O'Leary completes his MBA at the University of Western Ontario.
  • 1983-O'Leary co-founded Special Event Television, a production company that produced original sports programming
  • 1986-O'Leary co-founded Softkey Software Products, a software company, with business partners John Freeman and Gary Babcock
  • 1995-Mattel Inc. acquires Softkey for $3.7 billion, with O'Leary remaining as the head of the company.
  • 2003-O'Leary begins to gain widespread recognition as a co-host on the business news program "SqueezePlay."
  • 2006-O'Leary publishes his first book, "Cold Hard Truth: On Business, Money & Life."
  • 2008-O'Leary joins the cast of the reality TV show "Shark Tank," becoming one of the original "sharks" on the show.
  • 2011-O'Leary launches O'Leary Funds, a mutual fund company.
  • 2014-O'Leary announces his candidacy for the leadership of the Conservative Party of Canada.
  • 2017-O'Leary withdraws from the Conservative Party leadership race.
  • 2019-O'Leary announces his departure from "Shark Tank" after nine seasons on the show.
  • 2021-O'Leary continues his business ventures, investments, and public appearances

"Money may go to bad people but it never goes to bad ideas"

-- Kevin O'Leary aka Mr. Wonderful
